Acacetin, (C16H12O5), flavone, Linarigenin, O-methylated flavone, (4-methoxy-5,7-dihydroxyflavone), antiperoxidative, antiinflammatory, antiaromatase
It blocks oestrogen synthesis, antiproliferative, induces apoptosis of cancer cells.
It is used in traditional Chinese medicine and by athletes and body-builders as dietary supplement to maintain testosterone levels and prevent increasing estrogen levels, relief from menopause symptoms, tumour growth prevention, reduction of estrogen synthesis compound.
It occurs in Robinia pseudoacacia black locust, in Betula pendula, silver birch tree, in Damiana.
