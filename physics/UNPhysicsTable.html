Bell wire
Bell wire is copper wire, usually solid, not stranded, soft-drawn, not hard-drawn, used in low current, low voltage applications.
Bezel
A bezel is a metallic encasing shiny rim, on jewellery, front of TV and VDU screen, CD and DVD drive.
A magnetic compass may have a rotating glass on bezel ring across the face of the compass that rotates 360o and is marked off in 3o notches for easy computation.
